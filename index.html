Our Mission

Asian Creative Network is a group dedicated to Asians who have dared to break the mold. This group is here for those of us who have found empowerment through the arts, and who continue to persevere in our creative paths, defying the expectations placed on us by both family and society. Whether your journey has been easy going so far, or you have already gone through some of the most difficult seasons of your life, this community is here to support you.
